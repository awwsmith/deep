Title-Page Features

Title: Londini Artium & Scentiarum Scaturigo. OR, Londons Fountaine of Arts and Sciences. Exprest in sundry Triumphs, Pageants, and Showes, at the Initiation of the Right Honorable NICHOLAS RAYNTON into the Maiorty of the famous and farre renowned City LONDON.
