Title-Page Features

Title: THE TEMPLE OF LOVE. A Masque.
Author: By Inigo Iones, Surveyour of his Majesties Workes; and William Davenant, her Majesties Servant.
Performance: Presented by the QVEENES Majesty, and her Ladies, at White-hall on Shrove-Tuesday, 1634.
Imprint: LONDON: Printed for Thomas Walkley, and are to be sold at his Shop neare White-hall. 1634.
