Variants: There are two issues of this edition. In Greg 774a(i), the title page lists Wilson as printer and no author attribution. In Greg 774a(ii), the original title leaf was replaced by a complete quire a8, which contains a new title leaf listing D'Ouvilly as author and no printer [a2r], a dedication [a3r], and commendatory verses [a5v]. See also Greg 774a(i)

Title-Page Features

Title: THE False Favourite DISGRAC'D. And, The REWARD OF LOYALTY. A Tragi-Comedy.
Author: Penned by George Gerbier D'Ouvilly, Esq.
Performance: Never Acted.
Imprint: LONDON, Printed for Robert Crofts, and are to be sold at his Shop, at the Crown in Chancery lane, under Sergeants Inne. 1657.

Paratextual Material

Dedication: Epistle to: Aubrey de Vere, 10th Earl of Oxford; William Craven, Earl of Craven; and John Belasyse [Bellasis], 1st Baron Belasyse of Worlaby; from: George Gerbier D'Ouvilly [a3r]
Commendatory Verses: 5: by "James Howel"; "E. Aldrich"; "Tho. Revel"; "A: Prissoe"; "J. Cole" [a5v]
Character List: "The Persons" [A2r]
