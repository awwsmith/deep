Title-Page Features

Title: The Order and Solemnitie of the Creation of the High and mightie Prince HENRIE, Eldest Sonne to our sacred Soueraigne, Prince of Wales, Duke of Cornewall, Earl of Chester, &c. ... Whereunto is annexed the Royall Maske,
Author:
Performance: As it was celebrated in the Parliament House, on Munday the fourth of Iunne last past. Together with the Ceremonies of the Knights of the Bath, and other matters of speciall regard, incident to the same. ... presented by the Queene and her Ladies, on Wednesday at night following.
Imprint: Printed at Britaines Bursse for Iohn Budge, and are there to be sold. 1610.
