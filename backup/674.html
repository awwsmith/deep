Title-Page Features

Title: A KING AND NO KING.
Author: Written by { FRANCIS BEAVMONT | & | JOHN FLETCHER } Gent.
Performance: Acted at the Black-Fryers, by his MAIESTIES Servants.
Imprint: And now the fifth time Printed, according To the true Copie. ... The STATINOR to DRAMATOPHILUS. [in single column] A Play and no Play, who this Book shall read, | Will judge, and weep, as if 'twere doue indced LONDON, Printed for William Leak, and are to be sold at his shop at the signeof the Crown in Fleet-street, between the two temple Gates. 1655.
