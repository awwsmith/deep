Title-Page Features

Title: ANDRIA The first Comoedie ... in English. A furtherance for the attainment vnto the right knowledge, & true proprietie, of the Latin Tong. And also a commodious meane of help, to such as haue forgotten Latin, for their speedy recouering of habilitie, to vnderstand, write, and speake the same.
Author: of Terence, ... Carefully translated out of Latin, by Maurice Kyffin.

Latin Motto: Haud fraustrà Spero. Comœdia, Imitatio Vitæ: Speculum Consuetudinis: Imago Veritatis. Cic.
