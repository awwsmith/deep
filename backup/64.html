Title: An Enterlude called Lusty Iuuentus. Liuely describyng the frailtie of youth: of nature, prone to vyce: by grace and good councell traynable to vertue.
Author:
Performance: [in single column] The Personages that speake, | Messenger. | Lusty Iuuentus | Good councell. | Knowledge. | Sathan the Deuyl. | Hypocrisie. | Felowshyp. | Abhominable liuing. | Gods mercyfull promyses | Foure may play it easely, takyng such partes as they thinke best: so that any one tak of those partes that be not in place at once.
