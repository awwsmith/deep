Title-Page Features

Title: THE PASSIONATE LOVERS, A TRAGI-COMEDY. The First and Second Parts.
Author: Written by LODOWICK CARLELL, Gent.
Performance: Twice presented before the KING and QuEENS Majesties at Somerset-House, and very often at the Private House in Black-Friars, with great Applause, By his late MAJESTIES Servants.
Imprint: LONDON, Printed for Humphrey Moseley, and are to be sold at his shop at the sign of the Prince's Arms in St. Pauls Church-yard. 1655.
