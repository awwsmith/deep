Title-Page Features

Title: Descensus Astrææ. THE DEVICE of a Pageant, borne before M. William Web, Lord Maior of the Citie of London ... Wherevnto is annexed A Speech deliuered by one clad like a Sea Nymph, who presented a Pinesse on the water brauely rigd and mand, to the Lord Maior, at the time he tooke Barge to go to Westminster.
Author: Done by G. Peele Maister of Arts in Oxford.
Performance: on the day he tooke his oath, beeing the 29. of October. 1591.
