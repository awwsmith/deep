Title-Page Features

Title: CRAFTIE CROMWELL: OR, OLIVER ordering our New State. A Tragi-Comedie. Wherein is discovered the Trayterous undertakings and proceedings of the said NOL, and his Levelling Crew. ... [in single column] Shall Cromwell not be famous made | Unto the after-times, | Who durst a THRONE for to invade, | And Act the worst of Crimes? | Shall not his Nose DOMINICALL, | In Verse be celebrated? | Shall famous HARRY MARTIN fall, | And not be nominated? | Shall PRIDE the Drayman, JOICE the Taylor, | And all the Holy Crew; | With HAMMOND, now K. CHARLES his Jaylor, | And STAINS, that Holy Jew, | Be read hereafter? (sure they shall) | And if my Muse give ayd, | This shall be their Memoriall, | THESE ROGUES THEIR KING BETRAYD.
Author: Written by MERCURIUS MELANCHOLICUS.
