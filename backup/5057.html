Variants: There are two issues of this collection. The first issue, dated 1602, exists in two states, varying in the imprint: STC 15681.5 contains the phrase "CVM PRIVILEGIO REGIS"; STC 15681 does not. The second issue, STC 15682, dated 1604, contains a cancel title leaf that reprints in English the Scots title to the edition of "The warkis" printed by Charteris in 1597 [STC 15664 and 15644.3]; it also includes a statement that the works are "Newly corrected," a new Latin motto, a new cut of the royal arms, and a new bookseller in imprint. See also STC 15681; 15682
