Title-Page Features

Title: Sidero-Thriambos. Or Steele and Iron Triumphing. Applauding the aduancement of Sir Sebastian Haruey, Knight, to the dignitie of Lord Maior of London. Taking his oath in the same authoritie at Westminster,
Author: Deuised and written by A. M. Citizen and Draper of London.
Performance: on Thursday, being the 29. day of October. 1618. Performed in hearty loue to him, and at the charges of his kinde Brethren, the right Worshipfull Company of Ironmongers.
