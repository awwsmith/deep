Title-Page Features

Title: THE RIVALL FRIENDS. A Comœdie,
Author: by The Authour, PET. HAVSTED Mr. in Artes in Queenes Colledge.
Performance: As it was Acted before the King and Queens Maiesties, when out of their princely favour they were pleased to visite their Vniversitie of Cambridge, upon the 19. day of March. 1631. Cryed downe by Boyes, Faction, Envie, and confident Ignorance, approv'd by the judicious, and now exposed to the publique censure,
Latin Motto: Non tanti est ut placeam insanire.
Imprint: LONDON, Printed by Aug. Matthewes for Humphrey Robinson, at the signe of the three Pidgeons in Pauls Church-yard. 1632.
