Additional Notes: The masque was performed on Feb 18, 1634, so the date in the performance attribution on the title page follows the legal reckoning. The end of the text states that "The Songs and Dialogues of this Booke were set with apt Tunes to them, by Mr. Henry Lawes, one of His Majesties Musitians" [S4v].
