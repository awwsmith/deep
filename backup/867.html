Title-Page Features

Title: THE ELDER BROTHER: A COMEDIE.
Author: Written by { FRANCIS BEAVMONT, | AND | JOHN FLETCHER. } Gent.
Performance: Acted at the private house in Blacke Fryers, with great Applause, by His late Majesties Servants.
Imprint: Printed according to the true Copie. ... The second Edition, Corrected and Amended. LONDON, Printed for Humphrey Moseley, and are to be sold at his Shop at the Princes Armes in St. Paules Church yard. 1651.
