Variants: There are two issues of this collection, both with the same title page and Wing number (S3486). The first issue contains the original six plays in the collection; the second issue adds The Gentleman of Venice (Greg 747a[i]) and The Politician (Greg 752a[i]). See also the other Wing S3486
 
Collection contains: The Brothers (The Politic Father); The Sisters; The Doubtful Heir (Rosania, or Love's Victory); The Imposture (The Impostor); The Cardinal; The Court Secret; The Gentleman of Venice; The Politician

Performance: The Five first were acted at the Private House in BLACK FRYERS with great Applause. The last was never Acted.
