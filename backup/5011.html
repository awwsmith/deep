Variants: The inner form of sheet * exists in two states, with distinct settings that affect the list of contents and the heading of the dedicatory epistle. Both are listed under STC 5232, though Greg distinguishes them as 66a(*) and 66a(†) (erroneously implying that the variants in the preliminaries of the collection are variant states of The Entertainment in the City of Bristow). In one issue, which corresponds to Greg 66a(*), the dedication is addressed to the "right worshipful" Christopher Hatton; in the other, which corresponds to Greg 66a(†), Hatton is called the "righte worshipfull." See also the other STC 5232
