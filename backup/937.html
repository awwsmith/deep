Title-Page Features

Title: Londini Status Pacatus: OR, LONDONS Peaceable Estate. Exprest in sundry Triumphs, Pageants, and Shewes, at the Innitiation of the right Honourable HENRY GARWAY, into the Majoralty of the Famous and farre Renowned City LONDON.
Author: Written by THOMAS HEYWOOD.
Performance: All the Charge and Expence, of the laborious Projects both by Water and Land, being the sole undertakings of the Right Worshipfull Society of Drapers.
Latin Motto: Redeunt Spectacula
Illustration: Three small woodcuts of the Drapers' arms
Imprint: Printed at London, by Iohn Okes. 1639.
