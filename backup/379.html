Title: THE TRVE DISCRIPtion of a Royall Masque.

Additional Notes: In the dedication to the second edition of this masque (Greg 207b), Daniel calls this an unauthorized edition published because of "the vnmannerly presumption of an indiscreet Printer, who without warrant hath divulged the late shewe at Court, presented the 8. of Ianuary, by the Queenes Maiestie and her Ladies, and the same verie disorderly set forth" (A3r).
