Title-Page Features

Title: THE excellent Cõmedie of two the most faithfullest friends, Damon and Pithias.
Author: Made by Maister EDWARDS, then being Maister of the Children.
Performance: as the same was shewed before the Queenes Maiestie, by the Children of her Graces Chappell, excepting (only) the Prologue which is somwhat altered for the proper vse of them that hereafter shal haue occasion to play it, either in priuate, or open Audience.
Imprint: Newly Imprinted, ... Imprinted at Londõ, by Richarde Iones: dwelling neere vnto Holborne Bridge, ouer against the signe of the Faulcon. Anno 1582.
