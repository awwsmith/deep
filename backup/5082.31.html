Stationer Information

Entries in Stationers' Register: Nov 8, 1623: Entered to Edward Blount and Isaac Jaggard: "Tragedies. ... Mackbeth".
Jun 19(?), 1627: Transferred from the widow of Isaac Jaggard to Thomas Cotes and Richard Cotes: "her parte in Shackspheere playes."
Nov 16, 1630: Transferred from Edward Blount to Robert Allott, by a note of 26 June: "Mackbeth."
Jul 1, 1637: Transferred from the widow of Robert Allott to John Legat (2) and Andrew Crooke (1) (by order of a court): "saluo Iure cuiuscunque ... Shakespeares workes their Part."
 
Additional Notes: For Middleton's revision of the play in Autumn 1616, see Gary Taylor, "Macbeth (adaptation)," in Thomas Middleton and Early Modern Textual Culture, 383-98.
