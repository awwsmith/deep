Variants: There are two issues of this collection. The title page for STC 11636 is printed in a border compartment and lists Bynneman as printer and Smith as publisher. STC 11637 contains a cancel title leaf, which is printed within a lace border and rules, lists Smith as publisher and no printer, and includes Smith's device [186]. The two title pages also differ in their formatting. See also STC 11636
