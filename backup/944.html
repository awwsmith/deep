Additional Notes: The masque was performed on Jan 21, 1640, so the title page dates follow the legal reckoning. The end of the text states that "The Invention, Ornament, Sceans and Apparitions, with their Descriptions, were made by Inigo Iones, Surveyor Generall of his Majesties Workes. What was spoken or sung, by William Davenant, her Majesties Servant. The Subject was set downe by them both. The Musicke was composed by Lewis Richard, Master of her Majesties Musicke" [D4v].
