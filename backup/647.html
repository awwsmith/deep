Title-Page Features

Title: CIVITATIS AMOR. The Cities Loue. ... At the ioyfull receiuing of that Illustrious Hope of Great Britaine, the High and Mighty CHARLES, To bee created Prince of Wales, Duke of Cornewall, Earle of Chester, &c. Together with the Ample Order and Solemnity of his Highnesse creation, ... As also the Ceremonies of that Ancient and Honourable Order of the Knights of the Bath; And all the Triumphs showne in honour of his Royall Creation.
Author:
Performance: An entertainement by water, at Chelsey, and White-hall. ... as it was celebrated in his Maiesties Palace of White-hall on Monday, the fourth of Nouember. 1616.
Imprint: LONDON, Printed by Nicholas Okes for Thomas Archer, and are to be sold at his shop in Popes-head-Pallace. 1616.
