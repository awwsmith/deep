Variants: There are two issues of this edition, varying in the imprint. Greg 204d(*) is dated 1615, and lists Basse's shop "vnder S. Butolphes Church." The second issue is dated 1616, and exists in two states: Greg 204d(†) lists Basse's shop "vnder S. Butolphes Church"; Greg 204d(§) lists it "vnder S. Butolphes Church without Aldersgate."
