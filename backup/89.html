Title-Page Features

Title: The excellent Comedie of two the moste faithfullest Freendes, Damon and Pithias.
Author: Made by Maister Edwards, then beynge Maister of the Children.
Performance: as the same was shewed before the Queenes Maiestie, by the Children of her Graces Chappell, except the Prologue that is somewhat altered for the proper vse of them that hereafter shall haue occasion to plaie it, either in Priuate, or open Audience.
Imprint: Newly Imprinted, ... 1571. Imprinted at London in Fleetelane by Richard Iohnes, and are to be solde at his shop, ioyning to the Southwest doore of Paules Churche.
