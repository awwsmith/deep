Title-Page Features

Title: A new Enterlude, ... entreating of the Life and Repentaunce of Marie Magdalene: not only godlie, learned and fruitefull, but also well furnished with pleasaunt myrth and pastime, very delectable for those which shall heare or reade the same.
Author: Made by the learned clarke Lewis Wager.
Performance: The names of the Players. [in double columns] Infidelitie the Uice. The Lawe. | Marie Magdalene. Knowledge of sinne. | Pride of life. Christ Iesus. | Cupiditie. Fayth. | Carnall Concupiscence. Repentaunce. | Simon the Pharisie. Iustification. | Malicious Iudgement. Loue. [in single column, centered below others] Foure may easely play this Enterlude.
Imprint: neuer before this tyme imprinted, ... Imprinted at London, by Iohn Charlewood, dwelling in Barbican, at the signe of the halfe Eagle and the Key. Anno. 1566.
