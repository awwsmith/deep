Title-Page Features

Title: PLEASANT DIALOGVES AND DRAMMA'S, ... With sundry Emblems ... As also certaine Elegies, Epitaphs, and Epithalamions or Nuptiall Songs; Anagrams and Acrosticks; With divers Speeches (upon severall occasions) ... With other Fancies
Author: SELECTED OVT OF LUCIAN, ERASMUS, TEXTOR, OVID, &c. ... extracted from the most elegant Iacobus Catsius. ... translated from BEZA, BUCANAN, and sundry Italian Poets. By THO. HEYWOOD.
