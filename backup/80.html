Title-Page Features

Title: A newe mery and wittie Comedie or Enterlude, ... treating vpon the Historie of Iacob and Esau, taken out of the .xxvij. Chap. of the first booke of Moses entituled Genesis.
Author:
Performance: The partes and names of the Players who are to be consydered to be Hebrews and so should be apparailed with attire. [in double columns reading vertically, joined by pointed brackets] 1 The Prologe, a Poete. | 2 Isaac, an olde man, father to Iacob & Esau. | 3 Rebecca an olde womā, wife to Isaac. | 4 Esau, a yong man and a hunter. | 5 Iacob, a yong mā of godly conuersation. | 6 Zethar a neighbour. | 7 Hanan, a neighbour to Isaac also. | 8 Ragau, seruaunt vnto Esau. | 9 Mido, a little Boy, leading Isaac. | 10 Deborra, the nurse of Isaacs Tente. | 11 Abra, a little wench, seruāt to Rebecca.
Imprint: newely imprinted, ... Imprinted at London by Henrie Bynneman, dwelling in Knightrider streate, at the signe of the Mermayde. Anno Domini. 1568.
