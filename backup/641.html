Title-Page Features

Title: Chrysanaleia: THE GOLDEN FISHING: Or, Honour of FISHMONGERS. Applauding the aduancement of Mr. IOHN LEMAN, Alderman, to the dignitie of Lord Maior of LONDON. Taking his Oath in the same authority at Westminster,
Author: Deuised and written by A. M. Citizen and Draper of LONDON.
Performance: on Tuesday, being the 29. day of October. 1616. Performed in hearty loue to him, and at the charges of his worthy Brethren, the ancient, and right Worshipfull Company of FISHMONGERS.
Imprint: Printed at London by George Purslowe. 1616.
