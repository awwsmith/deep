Title-Page Features

Title: A pleasant Enterlude, intituled, Like will to Like quoth the Deuill to the Collier. Wherin is declared what punishments followe those that will rather liue licentiously: then esteeme and followe good Councell. And what benefits they receiue that apply them selues to vertuous liuing and good exercises.
Author: Made by Vlpian Fulwel.
