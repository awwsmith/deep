Title-Page Features

Title: THE Tragedie of King Richard the second.
Author: By William Shake-speare.
Performance: As it hath beene publikely acted by the Right Honourable the Lord Chamberlaine his seruants.
Imprint: LONDON, Printed by W. W. for Mathew Law, and are to be sold at his shop in Paules Church-yard, at the signe of the Foxe. 1608.
