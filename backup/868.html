Entries in Stationers' Register: Mar 24, 1637: Entered to John Waterson and John Benson: "a Comedy called the Elder Brother written by Iohn ffletcher".
Oct 31, 1646: Transferred from John Benson to Humphrey Moseley (half): "these two Playes vizt. The Elder Brother. by mr Flesher. halfe".
Oct 31, 1646: Transferred from John Waterson to Humphrey Moseley (his part): "these Playes following (vizt) The Elder Brother his part ... by Mr fflesher".
 
Additional Notes: This was probably a pirated edition issued by Francis Kirkman and printed by Thomas Johnson; see Johan Gerritsen, "The Dramatic Piracies of 1661," Studies in Bibliography 11 (1958): 117-31. We have retained it in DEEP because it may have appeared prior to the end of 1660.
