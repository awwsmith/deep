Variants: There are three issues of this edition. The first issue, dated 1641, exists in two states: Greg 246b(i) does not list the address of Lunne’s bookshop, while Greg 246b(ii) does. The second issue, Greg 246b(iii), dated 1646, lists the address of Lunne’s shop and a different printer. The third issue, Greg 246b(iv), dated 1657, contains a different author attribution and lists Kirton as publisher. See also Greg 246b(i); 246b(iii); 246b(iv)

Title-Page Features

Title: Bussy D'Ambois: A TRAGEDIE:
Author: Being much corrected and amended by the Author before his death.
Performance: As it hath been often Acted with great Applause.
Imprint: LONDON: Printed by A. N. for Robert Lunne, and are to be sold at his house next doore to the signe of the Crane on Lambeth Hill at the end of Old Fishstreet. 1641.
