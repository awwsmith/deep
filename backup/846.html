Additional Notes: The masque was performed on Feb 24, 1636, so the title page dates follow the legal reckoning. The end of the text states that "The Musick of the Songs and Symphanies were excellent compos'd by Mr. Henry, and Mr. William Laws, his Majesties Servants" [C4r].
