Title-Page Features

Title: THE COMMODY OF the moste vertuous and Godlye Susanna,
Author: Compiled by Thomas Garter.
Performance: [in single column] Eyght persons may easyly play it. | 1. The Prologue and the Gaylour for one. | 2. Ioachim and Iudex for another, | 3. Sathan and Uoluptas another, | 4. Sensualitas alone. | 5 Susanna alone. | 6. Helchia, True Report, Ancilla, another, | 7. Ill Reporte the Uyce, and Cryer, another. | 8. Helchias wyfe, Danyell, Seruus, Serua, for another.
Imprint: neuer before this tyme Printed. ... IMPRINTED AT London, in Fleetestreate, beneath the Conduite, at the Signe of S. Iohn Euangelist, by Hugh Iackson. 1578.
