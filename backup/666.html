Title-Page Features

Title: THE Maids Tragedy,
Author: Written by FRANCIS BEAUMONT and JOHN FLETCHER, Gentlemen.
Performance: As it hath been divers times Acted at the Black-Friers, by the KINGS Majesties Servants:
Illustration: Woodcut on title page of "Amintor" stabbing the cross-dressed "Aspatia"
Imprint: The sixth Impression, Revised and and Corrected exactly by the Original. London Printed for William Leake, at the Crown in Fleet-street, between the two Temple Gates. 1650.
