Title-Page Features

Title: WHEN YOV SEE ME, You know mee. Or the famous Chronicle Historie of King HENRY the Eight, with the birth and vertuous Life of EDWARD Prince of Wales.
Author: By SAMVEL ROWLY, Servant to the Prince.
Performance: As it was played by the High and Mighty Prince of Wales his Servants.
Illustration: Woodcut of King Henry the Eighth on title page
Imprint: LONDON, Printed by B. A. and T. F. for Nath: Butter, and are to be sold at his shop in St. Pauls Church-yard, neare St. Austins Gate. 1632.

Entries in Stationers' Register: Feb 12, 1605: Entered to Nathaniel Butter: "Yf he gett good alowance for the enterlude of .K. Henry the .8th before he begyn to print it. and then procure the wardens handes to yt for the entrance of yt: He is to have the same for his copy".
May 21, 1639: Transferred from Nathaniel Butter to Miles Flesher: "saluo iure cuiuscunque ... The Interlude of king Henry the Eight."
