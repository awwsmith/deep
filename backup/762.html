Title-Page Features

Title: THE SPANISH BAWD, REPRESENTED JN CELESTINA: OR, The Tragicke-Comedy of CALISTO and MELIBEA. Wherein is contained, besides the pleasantnesse and sweetenesse of the stile, many Philosophicall Sentences, and profitable Instructions necessary for the younger sort: Shewing the deceits and subtilties housed in the bosomes of false seruants, and Cunny-catching Bawds.
