Additional Notes: The masque was performed on Feb 14, 1632, so the title page dates follows the legal reckoning. The end of the text states that the verses for the masque were "written by" Aurelian Townshend, and the subject, allegory, descriptions, and apparatus of the scenes "invented by Inigo Jones, Surveyor of his Maiesties worke" [C3v].
