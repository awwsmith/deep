Variants: There are two issues of this collection. STC 6238, dated 1623, includes both dramatic and non-dramatic texts by Daniel. STC 6243.8, dated 1635, is a reissue with a new general title page of quires 2A-2T, and contains all the plays in the earlier issue of the collection. See also STC 6243.8
 
Collection contains: Philotas; Hymen's Triumph; The Queen's Arcadia (Arcadia Reformed); The Vision of the Twelve Goddesses (The Masque at Hampton Court); Cleopatra
