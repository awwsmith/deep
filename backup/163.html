Title-Page Features

Title: THE Famous Chronicle of king Edwarde the first, sirnamed Edwarde Longshankes, with his returne from the Holy land. Also the life of Lleuellen, rebell in Wales. Lastly, the sinking of Queene Elinor, who sunck at Charing-crosse, and rose againe at Potters-hith, now named Queene-hith.

Aug 13, 1599: Transferred from Abel Jeffes to William White: "Edward Longshankes."
