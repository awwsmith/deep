Variants: There are two issues of this collection, both with the same Wing number [S6128]. The first was issued with no additional material; the second was issued with the 1659 collection The Last Remains of Sir John Suckling [Wing S6130], which includes The Sad One. See also the other Wing S6128

Additional Notes: There is also an imperfect copy of a later edition of Fragmenta Aurea, which lacks all leaves before N1. It is bound up with a copy of The Last Remains (1672?) but was presumably printed earlier and closely resembles the edition of 1658. This edition is not included in DEEP.
