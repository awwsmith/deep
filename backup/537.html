Variants: The outer form of sheet E exists in two states. In Greg 278a(*), the fourteen lines of the chorus on E4v are set in italic, the catchword on E4v is approximately correct, and a lacuna on E2v is indicated by a short rule. In Greg 278a(†), the fourteen lines of the chorus are set in roman, the catchword on E4v erroneously repeats that on E3r, and the lacuna is indicated by a blank. See also Greg 278a(*)
