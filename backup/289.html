Title: THE CHRONICLE History of Henry the fift, With his battell fought at Agin Court in France. Together with Auntient Pistoll.
Author:
Performance: As it hath bene sundry times playd by the Right honorable the Lord Chamberlaine his seruants.

Stationer Information

Printer: Creede, Thomas
Publisher: Pavier, Thomas
Imprint Location: O.9 (Royal Exchange - South Side)
Entries in Stationers' Register: Aug 4, 1600(?): "Henry the ffift: / a booke ... to be staied".
Aug 14, 1600: Entered "These Copyes followinge beinge thinges formerlye printed & sett over to" Thomas Pavier: "The historye of Henrye the vth wth the battell of Agencourt".
Aug 4, 1626: Transferred from the widow of Thomas Pavier to Edward Brewster and Robert Bird: "The history of Hen: the fift, and the play of the same".
Nov 8, 1630: Transferred from Robert Bird to Richard Cotes: "Agincourt."
 
Additional Notes: William Shakespeare: A Textual Companion dates the first production to 1598-1599; Annals dates it to 1599.
