Additional Notes: The imprint date follows the legal reckoning. The half-title reads [π4v]: "The misfortunes of Arthur (Vther Pendragons Sonne) reduced into Tragicall notes by THOMAS HVGHES one of the societie of Grayes-Inne. And here set downe as it past from vnder his handes and as it was presented, excepting certaine wordes and lines, where some of the Actors either helped their memories by brief omission: or fitted their acting by some alteration. With a note in the ende, of such speaches as were penned by others in lue of some of these hereafter following."
