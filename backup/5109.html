Variants: There are two issues of this collection, both of which exist in two states. The first issue, STC 18343, contains the original title leaf dated 1638, and lists "I. D." as printer, Green as publisher, and Fussell as bookseller; its preliminaries exist in two states: Greg 543a(i*)-544a(i*) is dedicated to Benedict Roberts, Greg 543a(i†)-544a(i†) to William Ball. The second issue, STC 18343a, contains a cancel title leaf dated 1639, and lists a different book title, "I. Dawson" as printer (and no publisher or bookseller), and a different author attribution; its preliminaries exist in the same two states: Greg 543a(ii*)-544a(ii*) is dedicated to Roberts, Greg 543a(ii†)-544a(ii†) to Ball. See also STC 18343; 18343; 18343a
