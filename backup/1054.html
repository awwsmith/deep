Title-Page Features

Title: THE JOVIALL CREW, OR, The Devill turn'd RANTER: Being a Character of The roaring Ranters of these Times. Represented in a COMEDIE, CONTAINING A true Discovery of the cursed Conversations, prodigious Pranks, monstrous Meetings, private Performances, rude Revellings, garrulous Greetings, impious and incorrigible Deporements of a Sect (lately sprung up amongst us) called Ranters. Their Names sorted to their severall Natures, and both lively presented in Action.
