Date of First Publication: 1639

Company of First Production: King's Men
Company Attribution: King's Men
 
Total Editions: 1 folio, 1 edition issued in both quarto (in collection) and octavo (single-play playbook)
 
Variants: There are two issues of this edition. Greg 568b(i) does not contain a title page, was printed as a quarto with 38 lines per page, and was intended to be part of the 1658 Mayne collection (Wing M1480). Greg 568b(ii) contains a title page dated 1659, was printed as an octavo with 35 lines per page, and was sold as a single-play playbook. The octavo issue was printed from the same setting of type as the quarto, the type being first rearranged and then reimposed; only the two prologues were completely reset. See also Greg 568b(i)
