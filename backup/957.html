Variants: There are three issues of this edition, each of which exists in variant states. Issue 1 contains the original preliminary half-sheet a4, the imprint for which lists Legat as printer. There are two settings of the inner form of sheet A: Greg 579a(i*) contains the readings "Herods" [A1v, line 8] and "conscious of" [A8r, line 15]; Greg 579a(i†) contains "Herods" and "conscious to." Issue 2, Greg 579a(ii*) and Greg 579a(ii†), the original half-sheet a4 was replaced by the cancel sheet a8, which adds commendatory verses [a5r], a list of errata [a8r], and "In the Annotations" [a8r]. This issue contains the same variant states of the inner form of sheet A. Issue 3, the title leaf of the second issue [a3r] was replaced by a cancel title leaf, which lists "I. L." as printer and Leake as bookseller. It contains the same variant states of the inner form of sheet A, and its title page also exists in two states: Greg 579a(iii*1,*2) and Greg 579a(iii†1,*2) list the play as a "TRAGEDIE"; Greg 579a(iii*1,†2) and Greg 579a(iii†1,†2) list the play as a "TRAGEDY." See also Greg 579a(i*); 579a(i†); 579a(ii*); 579a(iii*1,*2); 579a(iii*1, †2); 579a(iii†1,*2); 579a(iii†1, †2)
