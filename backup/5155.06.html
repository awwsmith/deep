Title-Page Features

Title: [(6)A1r] THE Court Secret, A TRAGI-COMEDY:
Author: WRITTEN By JAMES SHIRLEY.
Performance: Never Acted, But prepared for the Scene at BLACK-FRIERS.
Imprint: Never Printed before. LONDON, Printed for Humphrey Robinson at the three Pigeons, and Humphrey Moseley at the Prince's Armes in Saint Paul's Church-yard. 1653.

Entries in Stationers' Register: Sep 10, 1653: Entered to Humphrey Moseley: "a Play called The Court Secret, Written by Iames Shirley."
 
Additional Notes: This play was never acted before the theaters closed in 1642, but Shirley wrote it with the intention that it would be performed by the King's Men at the Blackfriars.
