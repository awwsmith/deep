Title-Page Features

Title: THE MASQVE OF THE INNER TEMPLE AND GRAYES INNE: GRAYES INNE AND THE INNER TEMPLE,
Author: By FRANCIS BEAMONT, Gent.
Performance: PRESENTED BEFORE his Maiestie, the Queenes Maiestie, the Prince, Count Palatine and the Lady Elizabeth their Highnesses, in the Banquetting house at White-hall on Saturday the twentieth day of Februarie, 1612.
Imprint: AT LONDON, Imprinted by F. K. for George Norton, and are to be sold at his shoppe neere Temple-bar.
