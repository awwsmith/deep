Title-Page Features

Title: A pretie Enterlude called Nice wanton. [in single column] Wherin ye may see, | Three braunches of an il tree: | The mother and her Children three, | Two naught, and one godly. | Early sharp, that wilbe thorne, | Soone il, that wil be naught: | To be naught better vnborne, | Better vnfed then naughtily taught.
