Title-Page Features

Title: THE TWO NOBLE KINSMEN:
Author: Written by the memorable Worthies of their time; { Mr. John Fletcher, and | Mr. William Shakspeare. } Gent.
Performance: Presented at the Blackfriers by the Kings Maiesties servants, with great applause:
Imprint: Printed at London by Tho. Cotes, for Iohn Waterson: and are to be sold at the signe of the Crowne in Pauls Church-yard. 1634.
