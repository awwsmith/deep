Title-Page Features

Title: THE DVTCHESSE OF MALFY. A Tragedy.
Author: Written by John Webster.
Performance: As it was approvedly well acted at the BLACK-FRIERS, By his Majesties Servants.
Latin Motto: [in single column] Horat.———Si quid——— | ———Candidus Imperti si non his vtero mecum.
Imprint: The perfect and exact Copy, with divers things Printed, that the length of the Play would not beare in the Presentment. ... LONDON; Printed by I. Raworth, for I. Benson, And are to be sold at his shop in St. Dunstans Churchyard in Fleetstreet. 1640.
