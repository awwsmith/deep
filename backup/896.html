Title-Page Features

Title: THE ROYALL MASTER;
Author: Written by IAMES SHIRLEY.
Performance: As it was Acted in the new Theater in Dublin: AND Before the Right Honorable the Lord Deputie of Ireland, in the Castle.
Latin Motto: ——Fas extera quærere regna.
Imprint: LONDON, Printed by T. Cotes, and are to be sold by Iohn Crooke, and Richard Serger, at the Grayhound in Pauls Church-yard. 1638.
