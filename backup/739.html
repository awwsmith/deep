Title-Page Features

Title: THE DESERVING FAVORITE. A TRAGI-COMEDY.
Author: Written by LODOWICK CARLELL, Esq;.
Performance: As it was presented before the KING and QUEENES Majesties at White-Hall, and very often at the Private house in Black-Friers, with great Applause. By his late MAIESTIES Servants.
Imprint: LONDON, Printed for Humphrey Moseley, and are to be sold at his shop at the Prince's Armes in St. Paul's Church-yard. 1659.
