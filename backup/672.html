Title-Page Features

Title: A KING, and NO KING.
Author: Written by { FRANCIS BEAMONT | & | IOHN FLETCHER } Gent.
Performance: Acted at the Blacke-Fryars, by his MAIESTIES Seruants.
Imprint: And now the third time Printed, according to the true Copie. ... The STATIONER to DRAMATOPHILVS. [in single column] A Play and no Play, who this Booke shall read, | Will iudge, and weepe, as if 'twere done indeed. LONDON, Printed by A. M. for Richard Hawkins, and are to bee sold at his Shop in Chancerie Lane, neere Serjeants Inne. 1631.
