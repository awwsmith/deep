Stationer Information

Entries in Stationers' Register: Nov 8, 1623: Entered to Edward Blount and Isaac Jaggard: "Comedyes. ... The Comedy of Errors".
Jun 19(?), 1627: Transferred from the widow of Isaac Jaggard to Thomas Cotes and Richard Cotes: "her parte in Shackspheere playes."
Nov 16, 1630: Transferred from Edward Blount to Robert Allott, by a note of 26 June: "Comodie of Errors."
Jul 1, 1637: Transferred from the widow of Robert Allott to John Legat (2) and Andrew Crooke (1) (by order of a court): "saluo Iure cuiuscunque ... Shakespeares workes their Part."
 
Additional Notes: William Shakespeare: A Textual Companion dates the first production to 1594; Annals dates it to 1592 [c.1590-1593].
