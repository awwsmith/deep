Stationer Information

Entries in Stationers' Register: Sep 17, 1658: Entered to Thomas Walkley: "The sad Shephard or a Tale of Robin hood. ... salvo jure cujuscunque."
Nov 20, 1658: Transferred from Thomas Walkley to Humphrey Moseley: "The Sad Shephard Or a Tale of Robin hood."
 
Additional Notes: There is no evidence that this play was ever acted--since it exists only as a fragment of just over two acts--but Jonson was apparently writing it with the intention that it would eventually be performed by a professional playing company.
