Title-Page Features

Title: THE MEMORABLE MASQVE OF THE TWO HONOVRABLE HOVSES OR Innes of Court; the Middle Temple, and Lyncolnes Inne.
Author: Inuented, and fashioned, with the ground, and speciall structure of the whole worke: By our Kingdomes most Artfull and Ingenious Architect INNIGO IONES. Supplied, Applied, Digested, and written, By GEO: CHAPMAN.
Performance: AS IT WAS PERFORMED Before the King, at White-hall on Shroue-Munday at night; being the 15. of Febr. 1613. AT THE PRINCELY CELEBRATION OF the most royall Nuptials of the Palsgraue, and his thrice gratious Princesse Elizabeth, &c. With a description of their whole show the Court, from the Master of the Rolls his house: with all their right Noble comsorts, and most showfull attendants.
Imprint: AT LONDON, Printed by F. K. for George Norton, and are to be sold at his shop neere Temple-barre.
