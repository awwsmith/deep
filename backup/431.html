Variants: There are two issues of this edition. Greg 232a(i) has no performance attribution and no date in the imprint. The second issue, Greg 232a(ii*) and Greg 232a(ii†), is dated 1607, lists different stationers, and advertises a performance by students of Trinity College at Oxford; it exists in two states, which differ in the punctuation and spelling on their title pages and in "The names of the Actors" [A1v]. See also Greg 232a(i); 232a(ii†)
