Variants: There are three issues of this edition. The first issue, dated 1657, lists Wilson as publisher and exists in two states; Greg 776a(i*) contains a blank dedication; Greg 776a(i†) is dedicated to "Mr. Francis Lenthall, junior." In the second issue, Greg 776a(ii), a slip was pasted over the original imprint, listing Pennycuicke as publisher and no date. See also Greg 776a(i†); 776a(ii)

Title-Page Features

Title: FANCY'S FESTIVALS: A Masque, ... with many various and delightful new Songs, for the further illustration of every Scene.
Author: Written by THO. JORDAN, Gent.
Performance: As it hath been privately presented by many civil persons of quality.
Latin Motto: Ficta, voluptatis causà, sint proxima veris. Hor. de Art. Poet.
Imprint: And now at their requests newly printed ... LONDON: Printed by Tho. Wilson at the sign of the Three-Foxes in Long-lane. 1657.
