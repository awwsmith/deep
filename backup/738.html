Title-Page Features

Title: The Deseruing FAUORITE.
Author: Written by LODOWICKE CARLELL, Esquire, Gentle-man of the BOWES, and Groome of the King and Queenes Priuie Chamber.
Performance: As it was lately Acted, first before the Kings Maiestie, and since publikely at the BLACK-FRIERS. By his MAIESTIES Seruants. [var.: "MAIESIES"]
Imprint: AT LONDON, Printed for MATHEW RHODES. 1629.
