Title-Page Features

Title: A KING AND NO KING.
Author: Written by { FRANCIS BEAVMONT | & | JOHN FLETCHER } Gent.
Performance: Acted at the Black-Fryars, by his MAIESTIES Servants.
Imprint: And now the fourth time Printed, according to the true Copie. ... The STATIONER to DRAMATOPHILUS. [in single column] A Play and no Play, who this Booke shall read, | Will judge, and weepe, as if 'twere done indeed. LONDON, Printed by E. G. for William Leake, and are to be sold at his shop in Chancery-lane, neere unto the Rowles. 1639.
