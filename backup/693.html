Title-Page Features

Title: THE TRAGEDY OF THIERRY King of France, and his Brother THEODORET.
Author: Written by { FRACIS BEAMONT. | AND | JOHN FLETCHER } Gent.
Performance: As it was diverse times acted at the Blacke-Friers, by the Kings Majesties Servants.
Imprint: LONDON, Printed for Humphrey Moseley, and are to be sold at his Shop at the Princes Armes in St. Pauls Church-yard. 1649.
